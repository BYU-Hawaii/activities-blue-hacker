Exploring the Islands of Samoa

Itinerary:

  1. Hike to the stunning Togitogiga Waterfall
  2. Snorkel in the crystal-clear waters of Lalomanu Beach
  3. Experience traditional Samoan culture at the Piula Cave Pool
  4. Visit the vibrant fish market in Apia

Packing List:

  • Sunscreen
  • Swimsuit
  • Hiking shoes
  • Reef-safe sunscreen
  • Light clothing for warm weather
